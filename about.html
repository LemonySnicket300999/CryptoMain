Technocrats

IT Club of SRDAV

Technocrats is the name of the IT club of our school, SRDAV Public School, which is the founder and organiser of Wartex. Technocrats has managed to host the annual wartex event since 2015. Launching with 12 events and participation from 350+ students from 18+ schools, we have come a long way to 850+ participants from 55+ schools for 18 events in Wartex #5.0 in 2019, with a few celebrities, such as Harsh Beniwal, Manoj Bakshi, Gaurav Arora, Pratishtha Sharma among others, gracing the event with their presence as well.
